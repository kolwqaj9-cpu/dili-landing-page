1. Information We Collect

DILI Data Analytics operates as a B2B data extraction service. We only collect basic professional contact information (such as corporate email addresses and names) when you directly communicate with us to request data samples or API access.

2. Our Data Sources

The structured Drug-Induced Liver Injury (DILI) datasets we provide are strictly derived from publicly available, peer-reviewed scientific literature and clinical databases. We do not collect, process, or store Protected Health Information (PHI) or Personally Identifiable Information (PII) of real-world patients.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your professional contact information is used solely to provide the requested data samples, respond to your inquiries, and maintain our B2B vendor-client relationship. We do not sell, rent, or trade your contact information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement standard industry protocols to ensure any B2B communication and data transfers are secure. However, no electronic transmission is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

5. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data processing practices, please reply directly to our outreach email.

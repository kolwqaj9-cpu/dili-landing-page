DILI Data Analytics

Accelerating Drug Safety Signaling through NLP

We provide specialized automated extraction of Drug-Induced Liver Injury (DILI) case reports from unstructured global scientific literature and clinical databases.

Our proprietary NLP pipeline converts complex medical texts into structured, machine-readable formats (JSON/XML) designed specifically for Pharmacovigilance and Drug Safety teams. We eliminate manual data processing, allowing your team to focus on toxicity signaling and risk assessment.

Key Data Points Extracted:

  • Patient Demographics (Age, Gender, Baseline Conditions)
  • Suspected Drugs and Concomitant Medications
  • Longitudinal Liver Biochemistry (ALT, AST, ALP, Total Bilirubin, etc.)
  • Clinical Outcomes and Causality Indicators
